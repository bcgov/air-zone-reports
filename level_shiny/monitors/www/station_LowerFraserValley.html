The Lower Fraser Valley has 6 locations where the Air Quality Health Index (AQHI) are reported. AQHI is a communication tool used to report the health impacts of poor air quality. It is calculated from the measured levels of fine particulate matter(PM2.5), ground-level ozone(O3), and nitrogen dioxide(NO2).
