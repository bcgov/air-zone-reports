Monitoring in the Coastal Air Zone

There are 7 air quality monitoring stations in the Coastal Air Zone that were active from 2019 to 2021. These stations were operated by Industry (5 stations), INDUSTRY-PRPA (1 stations), and the Ministry of Environment and Climate Change Strategy (ENV) (1 stations).

Terrace ENV PM2.5,O3,NO2,SO2

The Coastal has 2 locations where the Air Quality Health Index (AQHI) are reported. AQHI is a communication tool used to report the health impacts of poor air quality. It is calculated from the measured levels of fine particulate matter(PM2.5), ground-level ozone(O3), and nitrogen dioxide(NO2).
